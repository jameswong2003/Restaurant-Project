Treat Yourself to some of Rosalind's Finest Dishes

Cornmeal Blini with Caviar

Something magical happens in the mouth when the tiny caviar eggs burst as you roll them around with the even tinier grains of cornmeal. The blini are the size of silver dollars—you will only need about three per person, but if this dinner is truly romantic, perhaps the extra batter will come in handy for breakfast the next morning.

Cobb salad

This time in food history was the era of tableside preparations at restaurants too — seeing a baked Alaska set aflame next right from your seat was the pinnacle of class — and as such, the Cobb salad was chopped and mixed right in front of the diners who ordered them, rather than being prepped and put together in the kitchen. The spectacle was all part of the appeal.

Steamed Lobster

Biting through the flesh of this delicious sea creature, you will taste the muscular and tender flesh of the lobster. Cooked with butter and cilantro, your taste buds will be happy that this dish was ordered.

T-Bone Steak

One of the best cuts in the steak world, T-bone steak bring in the flavorful taste of the beef along with the bone. Heavily roasted on the grills, this steak offers tender meat with a side of fried onion rings. It truly is the king cut of all steaks.

Angel Hair with Chicken & Cherries

With the toppings of Chicken and Dried Cherries, this dish brings in a new unique flavors that has not been seen before. The cheddar Cheese springled one top will melt into the cheddar cream that will bring and mix all flavors together. This pasta combination will be among one of the most unique in which you will have a chance to taste.
