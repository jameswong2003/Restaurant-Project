Mission Statement

At Rosalind’s we take pride in our food. Chef and owner Rosalind Bind first started Rosalind’s with the idea of an elegant yet comfortable dining environment. Many of the chefs at Rosalinds have trained at Michelin star restaurants all over the country and we hope that our diners will savor the flavors that we meticulously blend in all of our dishes. We serve traditional dishes to hungry vistors. Our enticing and welcoming restaurant is open to all customer and ready to serve you. Enhancing your experience from the door to your table.
